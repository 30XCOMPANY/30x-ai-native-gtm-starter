# strategy/ · Who we are. Who we sell to. What we believe.

> The brain of the operation. Stable beliefs about your company, your market, and your moats. Other folders depend on these being filled in.

## Files in this folder

| File | When to open | Cadence |
|---|---|---|

| [`capability-radar.md`](./capability-radar.md) | Foundation-model obsolescence watch | **Weekly** event-triggered |

## Fill order (first-time setup)

If you're starting fresh, fill in this order:

1. **about.md** — facts about your company. Easiest, anchors everything else.
2. **positioning.md** — what you sell, to whom, why now.
3. **icp.md** — the AI-native ICP (LLM Maturity Stage × Data Residency × Spend Trajectory).
4. **personas.md** — at least one persona, expand later.
5. **competitors.md** + **capability-radar.md** — even rough versions are better than empty.
6. **pricing.md** — defer if you haven't decided.
7. **brand.md** — defer if you don't have brand guidelines yet; defaults work fine.

The `setup` skill (in `skills/setup/`) can pre-fill most of these from your public footprint.
