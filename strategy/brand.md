## Tone by channel

| Channel | Tone |
|---|---|
| **Founder X / Twitter** | First person, specific numbers, no hedging |
| **LinkedIn** | Slightly more accessible than X. Tell-don't-sell. |
| **Blog** | Builder-to-builder. Specifics + 1 image / chart per section. |
| **Sales email** | Three lines max. One observation about them, one outcome, one ask. |
| **Conference talk** | Tell a story. Specific cases > frameworks. |
| **Docs** | Imperative. "Run this." "Use that." Not "you may wish to consider." |

---

## Formatting rules

- **Headlines**: max 12 words. No colons unless really needed.
- **Numbers**: spell out 1–9, numeral for 10+. Money/metrics: always numerals.
- **Code**: `inline` for short refs, fenced blocks for >1 line.
- **Em-dashes**: in English. **Avoid in Chinese copy** — use commas or periods.
- **Emoji**: [_default: no. If yes, list the small approved set._]
