## How to use this folder

1. **For users of this repo**: read these before filling out your own `context/`. The death cases will tell you which traps not to fall into, the GTM teardowns will tell you which archetype to copy, the signals will tell you what to actually monitor.
2. **For Claude inside this repo**: when asked a strategic question, cite case examples from these files instead of inventing. Format: `(see research/01...md · Jasper)`.
3. **For forkers / contributors**: update these files when new cases appear. Death cases especially — every 6 months there will be new ones. Keep an `evolution log` style entry.

## What's NOT in here

- No proprietary data, no anonymized customer interviews, no NDA-bound information
- No prediction or forecasting. Cases are historical / cited.
- No vendor-paid research. All sources are public.

## Source quality tiers

We tag sources roughly:
- **Tier 1**: company's own statement (blog, SEC filing, founder thread with confirmed authorship)
- **Tier 2**: established industry publication (TechCrunch, The Information, Fortune, SaaStr, Sacra)
- **Tier 3**: secondary aggregation (Medium analysis, Substack write-up by named author)

When you cite from here in `outputs/`, prefer Tier 1 sources when available.
