Operating model

The shape of a real GTM org, mapped to folders.

Eight functional folders. Each with a clear entry point. Each tied to skills Claude can run. The architecture is the IP — your opinions live inside.

strategy/
Strategy

WHO we are. WHO we sell to. WHAT we believe. Positioning, ICP, personas, pricing, brand, competitors, capability radar.

Open firstREADME.md → about → positioning → icp
content/
Content

Demand gen through writing. Strategy, calendar, library, blog drafts, social threads.

Open firststrategy.md
pipeline/
Pipeline

Prospects and customers. Sales playbook, signal library, target accounts, outbound sequences, objections, customer roster.

Open firstplaybook.md
assets/
Assets

Branded outputs. Decks, one-pagers, illustrations, logos — all bound by visual rules.

Open firstdesign.md (visual brand system)
skills/
Skills

Eleven repeatable Claude actions. Setup, ICP scoring, deck-build, outbound sequencing, objection handling, drift audit, more.

Open firstsetup/SKILL.md
workflows/
Workflows

Decision trees for humans. OSS funnel, model launch response, integration partner outreach, agent-native distribution.

Open firstby scenario
playbooks/

Emergency response. Foundation model commoditizes your feature. Competitor ships. Viral moment. Capability obsolescence.

Open firstby event
sync/
Sync

MCP-first live data connectors. GitHub, Slack, Linear, Attio, OpenRouter, HF Hub — feeds the signal library.

Open firstREADME.md + mcp-servers.md
How it works

Claude reads the repo. You stop pasting context.

Clone it. Fill strategy/ once. Run GTM from a single prompt. Knowledge persists in git and grows weekly.

  • CLAUDE.md · 2-minute lobby. Claude reads this first.
  • strategy/ · positioning, ICP, personas, brand, competitors.
  • skills/ · 11 SKILL.md actions, from setup to weekly-drift-audit.
  • assets/design.md · binding visual rules for every deck and one-pager.
  • workflows/ + playbooks/ · decision trees and emergency scripts.
  • sync/ · MCP-first live data connectors.
